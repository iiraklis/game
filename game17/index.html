GAME INSTRUCTIONS

Objective

Survive as long as possible in space while avoiding asteroids and collecting bonuses. Your score increases over time and with each collected bonus.

Controls

  • Mouse: Move your mouse left/right to control the spaceship
  • Keyboard: Use LEFT ARROW and RIGHT ARROW keys to move the spaceship

Game Elements

  • Spaceship: Your vehicle. Avoid collisions with asteroids!
  • Asteroids: Dangerous space rocks that will damage your ship on contact
  • Energy Spheres: Green spheres that restore 1 health point when collected
  • Stars: Yellow stars that give you 50 bonus points when collected

Scoring System

  • Survival points: You earn points equal to your current level every frame
  • Star bonus: Each star collected gives you 50 points
  • Level up: Every 500 points you advance to the next level
  • Speed increase: Each level makes asteroids move faster

Tips for Success

  • Prioritize avoiding asteroids over collecting bonuses
  • Collect energy spheres when your health is low
  • Anticipate asteroid movement patterns
  • Stay near the center for better maneuverability
  • Watch for bonus spawns and plan your route
